Let`s Meet

I`m Iryna.

My name is Irina and I was born in a small village in Ukraine. Since my early years I’ve learned to take care of my siblings, including all the house work like cooking and cleaning. When I grew up I acquired the profession of a teacher, worked with children for many years and became more and more convinced that a healthy, full-fledged generation was directly related to family health, order in the house, and the cleanliness of the world. In 2007, I was fortunate enough to move to the United States. I was looking for the jobs in different industries. As the mother of two sons I realized that I can help families to live worry free lives in their everyday life journey by keeping their houses tidy and neat. Thus, in 2013, my husband and I registered our small business company names “ID Home Services Inc.” in state of Illinois, where I had the opportunity to meet wonderful people and to become part of their family, for which I am very grateful to them! Recently my family moved to Colorado Springs and we love everything about Colorado! I’m ready to meet new families and make their life a little easier by putting all my hard work to keep your house shiny happy!
